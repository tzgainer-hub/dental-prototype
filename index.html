What We Treat

Comprehensive Oral &
Maxillofacial Surgical Care

From routine extractions to complex reconstructive procedures, our surgeons offer the full scope of oral and maxillofacial surgery.

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacement that restores function and confidence. Single implants, full arch, and implant-supported bridges.

Wisdom Teeth Removal

Comfortable, safe extraction of impacted and erupted wisdom teeth with IV sedation options for a relaxed, pain-free experience.

Corrective Jaw Surgery

Orthognathic surgery to correct jaw misalignment, improving bite, facial appearance, and long-term oral health.

Bone Grafting

Jaw bone regeneration and augmentation to prepare the foundation for implants or restore bone lost to disease or injury.

TMJ & Facial Pain

Diagnosis and treatment of temporomandibular joint disorders, facial pain, and related conditions causing chronic discomfort.

IV Sedation & Anesthesia

Full IV sedation and general anesthesia services for a completely comfortable, anxiety-free surgical experience.
