✅ What's Included in a Revision

Each revision round covers any combination of the following. One round = one feedback submission + one set of changes made within 48 hours.

Color Scheme & Palette
Swap to a different palette option, adjust accent colors, or tweak overall tone. Full redesign of color variables across the site.
All Text & Copy
Headlines, doctor bios, service descriptions, taglines, CTAs, footer content — any written content on the site.
Services Listed
Add, remove, rename, or reorder services on the homepage and services section.
Doctor Bios & Photos
Update bios, swap or add headshots, adjust credentials displayed, reorder doctors.
Testimonials
Add, swap, or edit patient testimonials. Update attribution names and procedure labels.
Contact Info & Hours
Phone numbers, addresses, office hours, adding or removing a location.
Logo & Branding
Swap in your practice logo, update favicon, adjust how the brand name is displayed.
Booking & Scheduling Links
Update or add scheduling URLs, patient portal links, and call-to-action destinations.
Stats & Social Proof Numbers
Update "years in practice," patient counts, awards, certifications, and trust indicators.
Social Media & Footer Links
Add or update Instagram, Facebook, Google Business links, and footer navigation.
✕ Out of Scope — Requires New Quote
  • Adding entirely new pages (e.g. a full blog, before/after gallery page, separate team page)
  • Custom patient portal or payment system build-out
  • Third-party software integrations beyond scheduling link (e.g. PMS sync, insurance verification)
  • Complete structural layout change — e.g. switching from single-page to multi-page
  • Video backgrounds or custom animation sequences
  • SEO keyword research and content strategy (available as add-on service)
  • Content writing from scratch when no information is provided by the client
