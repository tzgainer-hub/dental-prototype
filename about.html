Advanced Technology

Surgical Precision Powered by
Cutting-Edge Tools

We invest in technology so our patients experience shorter procedures, faster recovery, and more predictable outcomes than ever before.

3D Cone Beam CT Imaging (CBCT)

Full 3D scans of your jaw, teeth, and sinuses in seconds — giving surgeons a precise roadmap before a single incision is made.

Digital Implant Planning Software

Every implant case is planned digitally before surgery — optimal position, angle, and depth mapped out with surgical-grade accuracy.

Surgical Microscope

High-magnification visualization ensures precision down to fractions of a millimeter — critical for complex reconstructive cases.

IV & In-Office Anesthesia

Full in-house anesthesia team — no hospital visit needed. We provide the same level of monitoring and safety as a licensed surgical center.
